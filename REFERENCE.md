## Design references (mechanics, not templates)
- **Restraint / editorial pacing:** Workshop Hospitality, Escape Coffee, Coffee Collective, Truekind Skincare — one bold moment surrounded by calm; type-led; warm neutral bases.
- **Fluid suds engine (mobile-proven):** Pavel Dobryakov WebGL Fluid Sim (paveldogreat.github.io/WebGL-Fluid-Simulation), Codrops WebGPU WaterBall — touch velocity injects force into a fluid field; suds part & re-close behind the finger.
- **Scroll-scrub timeline:** LX Hausys Trendship 2025, Done Drinks (GSAP) — scroll position scrubs a deterministic, reversible transformation = "scroll = one wash cycle."
- **Material-as-hero:** Luxury Fabrics House, Carnegie Fabrics — fabric/texture as the visual system.
- **Brand-verb-as-motion:** Move Hotels ("Stay in Motion") — the verb IS the motion brief; ours is **tumble**.

**Anti-cliché palette guidance:** kill bubbly cartoon-blue. Here we instead ELEVATE Sudzy's REAL red/white/stainless + Americana, treating red as cinematic editorial (deep, not primary) and the murals as authentic neighborhood texture — bespoke to THEM, not an imposed minimalism.
