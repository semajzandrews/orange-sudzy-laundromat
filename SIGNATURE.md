# SIGNATURE — Sudzy Laundromat

## World sentence
"6 AM on Main Street, stainless drums turning under a Statue-of-Liberty mural —
the cleanest, busiest corner in Orange, and it knows exactly when to send you in."

## The bet (chosen concept: "The Cycle, honestly timed")
Scroll IS the wash cycle. The page is one front-loader drum: as you scroll you
spin it — fill → wash → rinse → spin → done — with real angular momentum, suds you
can stir with a finger, and a timer counting down to a fresh-clean payoff. Threaded
through it is the one honest, useful truth from their reviews: **Sudzy is always
busy, so the site tells you the best time to come.** Craft spectacle + real utility.

## Fields
- **craft_verb:** tumble (the drum turning a load clean)
- **evidence:** Huebsch/Wascomat front-loaders in recon photos; "clean machines" in reviews
- **material:** water + suds + fabric; brand material = brushed stainless + cinematic red
- **state_change:** soiled→fresh; messy→folded; cycle fill→wash→rinse→spin→done
- **touch_primitive:** vertical scroll = drum angular velocity (inertia/wind-down);
  finger drag = stir the suds (WebGL fluid splat that re-closes behind the finger)
- **signature_arc:** build — scroll spins the drum up; a live cycle timer counts
  DOWN from the wash time and hits 00:00 exactly at the fresh-clean bloom
- **signature_position:** cover (the drum is the hero) + threaded as the scroll spine
- **second beat (the honest hook):** "Best time to come" — an hours-aware busy-rhythm
  meter ("Right now: packed · calmest weekday mornings") drawn straight from the
  reviews. Solves their #1 complaint; no competitor laundromat site does this.
- **cta_shape:** earned — completing the cycle opens the drum door to the CTA:
  "Drop it. We'll wash, weigh & fold." + smart-card promo ("add $20, get $5")
- **idle_behavior:** breathing — leave it alone and the drum keeps slowly tumbling
  (Move Hotels "stay in motion"); rewards the meditative wait, the real laundromat mood
- **completion_threshold:** user scrolls through one full cycle (or drags the suds ≥2x)
- **completion_cta:** "Your laundry, done by weight. Drop-off open 6–11."
- **fallback_chain:** [full WebGL fluid, CSS/canvas tumble, static before/after] 
- **reduced_motion_composition:** static drum + clean before/after + the busy-rhythm
  meter still works (it's data, not motion)
- **sound:** none (it's a phone in public) · **haptic:** optional soft tick at 00:00

## Palette / type (bespoke to Sudzy, not imposed)
- **Palette:** deep cinematic Sudzy red (not primary), warm laundry white/cream,
  brushed stainless, one cool foam-white highlight. Americana + tropical murals as
  authentic texture accents, not kitsch.
- **Type:** editorial serif display whose variable weight/width rides the spin
  velocity (the tumble made typographic) + clean grotesque body. No rounded "bubble" fonts.
- **Voice:** plain, proud, neighborly; English-first with Spanish-friendly touches
  (community truth). Honors the owner's real signage voice.

## Concept panel (judged)
1. **"The Cycle, honestly timed"** ← WINNER. Spin-physics cycle spine + the busy-rhythm
   utility beat. Spectacle that's also genuinely useful. Industry-first framing.
2. "Always Busy, Always Clean" — community-hub, people-first; busy-meter as hero.
   (Folded INTO #1 as the second beat rather than discarded.)
3. "Sudzy Standard" — Americana-pride editorial, mural/flag texture, institution-since-2010.
   (Folded into #1 as the palette/texture layer.)
